THE CHARACTERS

Kevin Flynn/Clu

Gifted computer programmer who created the best-selling video games for ENCOM. Created TRON - an arcade game based on his experience inside the Computer World. Began to focus all of his efforts on creating "a digital frontier to reshape the human condition". Played by Jeff Bridges.

Tron/Alan Bradley

A security computer program (written by Alan Bradley) and taken prisoner by the self-aware Master Control Program and forced to play on the Game Grid. He gained a reputation as a program who fought for the users. Played by Bruce Boxleitner.

Ed Dillinger

The main human antagonist. Worked at ENCOM as a progammer until he reacted Senior Executive because of his creation - the Master Control Program. Played by David Warner.

Sark

The secondary antagonist, a merciless and evil program. Written by Ed Dillinger. Commander of the MCP's army and overseer of the games fought on the Game Grid.
